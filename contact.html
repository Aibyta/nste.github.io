Frequently Asked Questions

You can browse facilities on our homepage and click "Book Facility" to reserve a slot.

Yes, bookings can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 2 hours before the start time.

Do I need to create an account?

Yes, an account is required to manage your bookings and access exclusive offers.

What payment options are available?

We support credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking via our secure payment gateway.
